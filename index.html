FEELMS

FEELMS is a website designed to recommend movies based on users' emotions or moods. By inputting their current feelings or desired emotional state, users receive curated film suggestions that align with their preferences, creating a more personalized and enjoyable movie-watching experience. As a film enthusiast, I developed FEELMS to enhance the way I choose movies based on my emotions. Recognizing its appeal, I designed the platform to benefit other film lovers who seek recommendations tailored to their mood.

ExaMPS

ExaMPS is an online website platform designed to streamline the process of creating, managing, and analyzing examinations. Developed specifically for educators, it simplifies test generation, administration, and result recording, including the Mean Percentage Score (MPS) for performance assessment.

This website was developed as part of our Practical Research 2 study, titled "Development and Testing of an Online Examination System with MPS Recording." It was presented at our school‚Äôs annual Research Congress, where our group was the sole representative from the TVL strand.
